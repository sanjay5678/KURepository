APPLICATION FOR SUBMISSION OF Ph.D THESIS.

EXTRACT OF RELEVANT RULES REGARDING SUBMISSION OF THESIS

  • A Candidate shall, on the completion of his work, submit to the Controller of Examinations.
  1. An Application on the prescribed form together with a Receipt for payment of the prescribed Fee for examining the Thesis / Dissertation.


  2. A Certificate signed by the Supervisor that his work and progress have been satisfactory and that in the opinion of the Supervisor the Thesis/Dissertation may be submitted for the award of the Degree of Doctor of Philosophy / Master of Philosophy.


  3. Submit Hard Copies of Ph.D Thesis at Examination Branch after Successfull Submission of Soft Copies
    (4 Thesis + 4 Synopsis)


  4. The Thesis shall be written in English provided, however, that when the Thesis on the Subject of other languages that English, It may be in the language itself or in English, When the Thesis is written in a language other than English, a detailed summary of the Thesis shall also be submitted along with it in English.
